Meet The Dogtors! We are an organisation of trained dogs and their human partners, visiting healthcare and social-service settings across Singapore. We care about connecting with people in a way that’s both relatable and easy to understand. Rather than leaving people puzzled, we wanted a name that immediately clicks—and maybe even makes you smile!
