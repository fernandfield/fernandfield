Why Community Education?

Outreach and education play a crucial role in fostering awareness, understanding, and action towards important societal issues, particularly those related to sustainability and environmental conservation.

ReThink Good hopes to effectively communicate key messages, raise awareness about pressing environmental challenges, and empower individuals to make informed decisions and take meaningful action.



As such, we provide free resources for the public for several reasons:

Accessibility

Making resources freely available ensures that everyone, regardless of socioeconomic status or geographical location, has access to valuable information and tools for sustainable living.

This lower barrier to entry promotes inclusivity and ensures that no one is left behind as we all work towards a more sustainable future.

Equity

Providing free resources helps level the playing field and reduces disparities in access to education and information.

It ensures that individuals from all backgrounds have equal opportunities to learn about and engage with sustainability practices, empowering them to make positive changes in their lives and communities.


If you are a student/youth group/initiative or an organization with limited financial resources or no budget allocated for workshops, we also offer subsidized or tailored sessions to accommodate your needs. Please use the button below to fill up the form and drop us an email for further details!

Impact

By removing financial barriers to access, free resources can have a greater impact on behaviour change and collective action. They enable more people to adopt sustainable practices, reduce waste, conserve resources, and contribute to positive environmental outcomes on a larger scale.

Public Good

Providing free resources for the public aligns with the principles of the common good and public service. It reflects a commitment to serving the broader community and advancing the well-being of society as a whole, rather than prioritizing profit or exclusivity.

Key Messages

We Hope to Spread:

Proper waste disposal prevents pollution and preserves natural resources.

Recycling and waste reduction conserve energy and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
Current systems not only result in waste being thrown into the environment but also necessitate the extraction and use of virgin materials, which eventually end up as waste too.
Everyone can play a part by adopting sustainable waste management practices.
