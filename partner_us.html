How do our bins work?

We understand the importance of flexibility and convenience in waste management solutions for corporate clients. That's why we tailor the pickup schedule to factors like location, usage patterns, and waste generation rates, with typical pickups scheduled weekly or bi-weekly. However, we remain flexible and can adjust pickup frequency as needed to accommodate specific demands.

Moreover, we continuously refine our services based on customer feedback and industry best practices, underscoring our commitment to delivering top-tier waste management solutions.

How does the Partnering work for collection points?

As for cost, our waste collection service offers transparent and flexible pricing plans tailored to meet the diverse needs and budgets of our clients. Factors such as the number of bins deployed, pickup frequency, location, and additional services required (such as sorting or recycling) are taken into account when determining costs. For detailed pricing information and personalized assessments, please reach out to our team for assistance.

How does the collections work?

We prioritize eco-friendly practices throughout our operations. This includes optimizing collection routes to minimize fuel consumption and emissions, utilizing electric or low-emission vehicles where possible, and ensuring that collected waste is managed and processed as circularity as possible with the least or zero waste.
