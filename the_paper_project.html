Handmade Recycled Paper & Seed Paper Project

Let's Talk About Handmade Recycled Paper and Seed Paper!

Handmade recycled paper and seed paper are sustainable alternatives to conventional paper.

They reduce the need for virgin paper, save trees, and minimize waste.

Seed paper, embedded with plant seeds, offers a unique way to give back to the environment when the paper is planted.

Join us in exploring these innovative materials and their benefits through our hands-on workshops.

Why Should We Use Recycled and Seed Paper?

Using recycled and seed paper supports sustainability by reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.
